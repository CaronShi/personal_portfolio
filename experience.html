Yelp-like Web Application

  • I have independently developed a responsive web application that bears similarities to Yelp's functionality. This platform serves as a business search with autocomplete, enabling users to seamlessly explore results. Users could effortlessly access essential business details, fostering informed decision-making. To provide unparalleled convenience, I incorporated the capability to visualize business locations on Google Maps. I also devised an efficient reservation feature, granting users the convenience of securing their spots. By seamlessly integrating this functionality, I enrich the user experience, ultimately contributing to the platform's appeal and usability.

    In the creation of this comprehensive web application, I harnessed a diverse array of cutting-edge technologies to ensure a seamless user experience and robust functionality, including Angular, Node.js, HTML, CSS, TypeScript, Javascript, Bootstrap, Restful API, and delopyed on Google GCP.


Pages' Detail & Functions

1. Business Search

Business search form is for collecting basic information for searching a business, including keywords, distance, category and location fields with form validation. Also, there are "Submit" and "Clear" button for users to either submit or clear the form.

Keywords Input with Autocomplete
The "Keywords" input field, enhanced with an autocomplete feature, empowers users to efficiently input relevant terms or phrases for their search. The autocomplete functionality predicts and suggests possible keywords as users type, saving time and reducing errors. This dynamic interaction refines the search experience, assisting users in finding accurate results effortlessly.

Location Input with Auto-detection
The "Location" input field not only allows users to manually input their desired location but also features an autodetection option. By clicking "Auto-detect my location" checkbox, the form leverages the ip.info API to detect the user's IP address and approximate geographical location. This seamless integration saves users from manually entering their location, providing real-time convenience. With this function, users can effortlessly receive results tailored to their current whereabouts, all while enjoying the benefits of autocomplete and other functionalities.



2. Search Results

After users submit the form, they will be presented with a comprehensive list comprising the top 10 relevant business results, meticulously sorted based on their proximity in a non-decreasing order. This well-structured table encompasses four essential columns, each delivering crucial insights. The first column showcases the index, providing a sequential reference for each entry. The second column elegantly displays the business logo or image, adding a visual element to the presentation. Moving forward, the business name is elegantly featured in the third column, granting users quick identification of the establishments. In the fourth column, users are promptly informed of the business's rating, offering valuable feedback on its quality. The fifth and final column of the table displays the distance to the business location, expressed in miles, procured from the Yelp RESTful API.


3. Business Details

Upon clicking a business result in the displayed table, users will access comprehensive details of this business showing in a Modal component. There are three subpages, business details, map, and reviews. The first page encompasses essential aspects such as the business's name, operational status, social media links, contact information, and three photos with fixed size using Carousel plugin. Also, an interative inserted Google Map is available in second subpage.



4. Revervation Form

Users could submit reservations directly from this web to businesses while benefiting from validation for accurate input. The form will feature an integrated date picker and time picker, enhancing user convenience. After successfully submit, an alert window will be pop-up for confirmation, then the "Cancel Reservation" button will show instead of "Reserve Now" button.



5. Revervation List

By clicking the "My Bookings" in navigation bar, the list of reservations collects all users' reservation submission in a structured table, and reservations could delete in this page at end of each row.
